Early Life & Education 

Juanpa Zurita was born on the 29th of March 1996 in Mexico City, Mexico. His parents’ names are Fernando Martínez Zurita (father) and Teresa Arellano (mother). He has three siblings, two brothers, Andrés Zurita, and Fernando Zurita and one sister, Paola Zurita. 

He schooled at local primary and high schools in Mexico City. He was a very brilliant student at school. He was known for always making good grades and making his family proud. 

Juanpa Zurita‘s family later moved to the USA when his father was appointed as a political counselor at the Mexican Embassy in Washington, D.C.

Juanpa Zurita became very famous when he posted his first video on Vine in 2013 titled That dog killed Chuk Norris And Is After Me He gained millions of followers on the vine after posting that. 

Career

Juanpa Zurita began his career as a content creator on the popular app Vine. He got millions of followers from the app. He published content that was in both fluent Spanish and English. By the time the app had closed in 2017, he had over 1.8 million followers. He then moved his content creation to Instagram and YouTube.

Juanpa Zurita then ventured into part-time modeling, where he thrived so well. He was the image of the firm Pull&Bear. In 2017 he collaborated with a Paris fashion show with Louis Vuitton, Dolce and Gabbana, and Fashion Week. 

He has also acted in some films, Audiovisuals’ and TV series. One of which is the series of the life of Luis Miguel.
