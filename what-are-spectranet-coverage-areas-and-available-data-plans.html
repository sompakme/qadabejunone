What are Spectranet coverage areas and available data plans

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Spectranet is one of the fastest growing internet service providers in Nigeria. But it is not available yet in all states and cities in Nigeria. This post provides the latest information available on Spectranet coverage areas in Nigeria.

Spectranet Nigeria offers affordable, fast and reliable broadband internet service and was the first internet service provider in Nigeria to launch a 4G LTE internet network. However, their internet broadband service is still yet to cover many cities. Spectranet started out in Lagos, but now they have expanded their coverage to some other cities. Currently, Spectranet covers the following list of cities in Nigeria:

  • Abuja
  • Ibadan
  • Lagos
  • Port Harcourt

Their head office is located at Plot 36B, Mobolaji Johnson Avenue Oregun Industrial Estate Alausa, Ikeja, Lagos.

For now, Spectranet internet coverage is still limited to the four cities listed above. Hopefully, they will extend their coverage to more cities and states very soon. See a list of their internet subscription plans available in these cities below.
