Despite the intense media scrutiny that comes with being a part of such a high-profile family, Paris has managed to carve out her own path in the entertainment industry. As of 2024, her net worth is a testament to her success in various fields, including modeling, acting, and music.

Early Life

Paris Jackson was born on April 3, 1998, in Beverly Hills, California. She is the second child and only daughter of pop legend Michael Jackson and Debbie Rowe. Paris has two brothers: Michael Joseph Jackson Jr., known as Prince, and Prince Michael Jackson II, known as Blanket.

Education

Paris attended the Buckley School, a private school in Sherman Oaks, California. After her father’s death in 2009, she and her brothers were placed under the guardianship of their grandmother, Katherine Jackson.

Modeling Career

Paris Jackson’s modeling career began in 2017 when she signed with IMG Models, a leading international modeling agency. She has since graced the covers of several high-profile magazines, including Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, and Rolling Stone.

Brand Endorsements

Paris has also landed lucrative endorsement deals with major brands such as Calvin Klein and Chanel, significantly contributing to her net worth.

Acting Career

Paris made her acting debut in 2017 on the Fox TV show “Star.” She has also appeared in films like “Gringo” in 2018 and “The Space Between” in 2021.

Music Career

Following in her father’s footsteps, Paris has also ventured into music. She formed a band called The Soundflowers with her then-boyfriend Gabriel Glenn. The duo released their debut EP in June 2020.

Real Estate Investments

Paris Jackson’s net worth is also bolstered by her real estate investments. She reportedly owns a $2.6 million house in Topanga, California.

Philanthropy

Like her father, Paris is known for her philanthropic efforts. She supports various charities, including the Elizabeth Taylor AIDS Foundation and Starlight Children’s Foundation.

Public Image

Despite her fame, Paris has faced her share of struggles, including battles with mental health and substance abuse. However, she has used her platform to raise awareness about these issues and advocate for mental health.

Personal Life

Paris is openly bisexual and has been in relationships with both men and women. She is also known for her eclectic style and numerous tattoos.

Future Prospects

With her diverse talents and the legacy of her father behind her, Paris Jackson’s net worth is expected to continue to grow in the coming years. She has several projects in the pipeline, including new music and acting roles.

Conclusion

Paris Jackson’s net worth of $25 million as of 2024 is a reflection of her successful career in modeling, acting, and music. Despite the challenges that come with her famous last name, Paris has managed to forge her own path and make a name for herself in the entertainment industry. With her talent and determination, there’s no doubt that her star will continue to rise in the future.
