Power Trip

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 6, 2024

Check out Power Trip, the latest track from J. Cole, featuring Miguel which dropped on Thursday, February 14th, 2013. J. Cole's movement grows stronger with each new release, and Power Trip has further strengthened that fact. It's a solid addition to the commendable body of work J. Cole has been creating since stepping foot in the rap game, and we're definitely looking forward to what J. Cole has on deck.

Hear more of J. Cole on Maine On Fire. Check out J. Cole's profile page for the most recent info, news, songs, and mixtapes.
