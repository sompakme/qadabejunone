What Time Will Netflix Release Stranger Things 3

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

It's been 614 days since the Season 2 release of Stranger Things and the Mind Flayer has been haunting you ever since. But don't worry—relief is coming.

Stranger Things Season 3 will premiere on Netflix on July 4, 2019 with eight new episodes. It already looks promising thanks to blossoming romances, Hawkins' brand-new mall, and a new monster from the Upside Down.

Related Story

The show will drop on Netflix at 12 a.m. PST on Thursday. For those of you on the East Coast who can't wait for sunrise to watch the new season, be sure to set your alarm for 3 a.m. EST. With the holiday weekend ahead, you'll have plenty of time to binge—and re-watch—before returning to the real world on Monday.

Watch on Netflix

Lucky for you, there aren't a million different streaming options for Stranger Things. When midnight strikes (or 3 a.m., East Coasters) just log into Netflix and enjoy.

For those of you who don't have a Netflix account yet, the streaming service offers three types of plans. The basic plan is $8.99 a month, while the standard and premium plans are $12.99 and $15.99, respectively.

The benefits for all three are basically the same—the most drastic difference is the number of screens you can watch Netflix on at the same time. The more expensive plans mean more screens, so if you plan on sharing your Netflix account with someone and don't want them interrupting your streaming of Season 3, you might want to upgrade.
