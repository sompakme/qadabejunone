Jessica Simpson reveals short haircut on Instagram

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 20, 2024

Jessica Simpson may be known for her tumbling blonde locks but the fashion designer decided to change up her look quite drastically over the weekend.Chopping off a good 12 inches of her hair, Jessica debuted a shoulder-length cut that was finished with a sharp edge.The singer-turned-entrepreneur seemed thrilled with her new 'do as she shared a photo on Twitter and Instagram with the caption: "Short hair don't care...".

PHOTOS TO ENLARGE

Jessica Simpson captioned her Instagram snap: 'Short hair don't care'

Jessica, 34, gave a special shout out to her celebrity go-to hairdresser Roque Jimenez – the man behind her wedding day hair and various red-carpet styles. She also owed her brighter shade of blonde to colourist Rita Hazan.In a second snap shared online, Jessica flaunted her clean look with another snap and pout, but this time included her new husband Eric Johnson, 35, in the background."I love him," she wrote of her former NFL player.

Jessica Simpson and her husband Eric Johnson married in July

Jessica and Eric, who are proud parents to two-year-old daughter Maxwell Drew and one-year-old son Ace Knute, married in July of this year.The couple had had to postpone their wedding due to the near back-to-back births of their children, and Jessica recently revealed that for the time being, she and Eric weren't planning on expanding their brood.The These Boots Are Made For Walkin' singer tied the knot with her athletic beau in a romantic ranch ceremony in California. Famous guests on the list included Jessica's younger sister Ashlee Simpson and Ashlee's then-fiancé Evan Ross, who she recently married in September.

Jessica Simpson chopped off her trademark blonde waves

"We have felt like ever since we got married, we've been kind of living on this honeymoon," Jessica told Ryan Seacrest last month.It certainly looks like the couple are still on cloud nine as Jessica regularly takes to social media to post photos of her and Eric looking as loved-up as ever. The sweethearts got engaged in 2010.
