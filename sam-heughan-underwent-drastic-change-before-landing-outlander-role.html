Sam Heughan underwent drastic change before landing Outlander role

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

The actor opened up about the transformation

    Outlander star Sam Heughan has revealed that he underwent a major change before landing his role on the hit Starz drama.
