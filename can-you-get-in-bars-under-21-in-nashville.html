Can You Get In Bars Under 21 In Nashville

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 3, 2024
People are currently reading this guide.

Nashville by Night: But Can You See the Honky Tonks After Dark? (If You're Under 21, That Is)

Let's face it, Nashville sizzles with live music, and those neon lights beckon like sirens. But if you're under 21 and have dreams of two-stepping through a crowded bar, you might need a little more than cowboy boots and a ten-gallon hat. Fear not, young grasshopper (or should I say, young catfish?), there's still fun to be had in Music City, even without the Jack Daniels.

The Age-Old Question: Are All Bars a Big, Fat "Nope" for Under 21?

Not exactly! Nashville's bar scene is a mixed bag. Many of the famous honky-tonks on Broadway switch to 21+ only after a certain time, usually around 6 pm or 8 pm. So, grab your crew and get your boots scootin' before then! Think of it as a pre-game for the real Nashville experience (minus the hangover, of course).

Honky Tonk Hacks: Sneaking In Under the Radar (Not Literally)

Here are a few pointers to navigate the Nashville nightlife scene as a minor:

  • Become a Line Dancing Machine: Many honky tonks, like the Wildhorse Saloon, offer free line dancing lessons before they go 21+. Brush up on your moves and become the envy of the dance floor (all ages welcome!).
  • Early Bird Gets the Worm (or Maybe Just the Live Music): Hit up Broadway before the dinner rush. You'll get a chance to experience the legendary atmosphere without the late-night crowds.
  • M****ake Nashville Your Stage (Literally): Plenty of venues, like The Listening Room Cafe, have separate areas for food and music. Grab a bite and enjoy the tunes without needing a drink in hand.

But Wait, There's More! Beyond the Honky Tonks

Nashville isn't just about bars! Here are some other cool options for the under-21 crowd:

  • Game Terminal: Channel your inner gamer at this epic arcade with classic and modern games. Think air hockey meets virtual reality.
  • Catch a Show: From comedy clubs to live music venues with all-ages policies, there's always something entertaining happening in Nashville. Just double-check the age restrictions before you head out.
  • Explore the City: Nashville has tons of museums, historical sites, and delicious restaurants to discover. Take a day trip to the Country Music Hall of Fame or grab some of the best hot chicken you'll ever have.

How-To FAQs for the Under-21 Nashville Adventurer

How to Find Out Which Bars Allow Under 21?

  • Check websites or social media pages of bars you're interested in.
  • Look for articles or reviews mentioning age restrictions.

How to Have Fun Without Alcohol?

  • Focus on the music! Nashville is a live music paradise.
  • Explore the city's unique offerings and hidden gems.
  • Embrace the friendly, energetic atmosphere.

How to Deal With Feeling Left Out?

  • Remember, there's a whole world of fun to be had in Nashville without alcohol.
  • Make the most of the all-ages options and create awesome memories.
  • Your time will come... and the honky tonks will still be there!

How to Convince Your Parents to Let You Out Late (Even Though You Can't Drink)?

  • Emphasize the safe and all-ages activities you plan to do.
  • Offer to check in regularly and stick to a pre-determined time to come home.
  • Maybe even bribe them with some souvenir Nashville hot chicken? (Just kidding... mostly.)
