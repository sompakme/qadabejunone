Plotlines & Story

The plot of The Story of a Low-Rank Soldier Becoming a Monarch revolves around Chris, a former soldier who lost his right hand and left eye in a war, and was reborn as a 15-year-old rookie with a mysterious artifact that grants him incredible power and potential. He embarks on a journey to become a monarch and protect his people from the threats of the world.

The story is set in a fantasy world where humans, orcs, elves, dwarves, and other races coexist. The world is divided into four continents, each ruled by a different monarch. The continent of Eria, where Chris lives, is plagued by war and chaos, as the human kingdom of Lancelot is invaded by the orc kingdom of Gorgon. Chris joins the Lancelot army as a low-rank soldier, and participates in the war against the orcs.

However, during a battle, Chris falls off a cliff and swallows his artifact, which he had no knowledge about. The artifact is a powerful device that grants him the ability to gain experience points and level up his skills, attributes, and abilities. He also gains access to a system that shows him his status, inventory, quests, and achievements. With the help of the artifact, Chris becomes a formidable warrior, and rises through the ranks of the army.

Along the way, Chris meets various allies and enemies, such as Guillien, a loyal friend and fellow soldier; Elise, a beautiful and talented mage; Remy, a cunning and charismatic spy; and Zane, a ruthless and ambitious general. He also encounters various challenges and dangers, such as monsters, bandits, assassins, and conspiracies. He also discovers the secrets and mysteries of his artifact, his rebirth, and the world.
