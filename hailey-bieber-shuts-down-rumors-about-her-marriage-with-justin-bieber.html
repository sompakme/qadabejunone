Hailey Bieber Shuts Down Rumors About Her Marriage With Justin Bieber

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 12, 2024

Hailey Bieber knows exactly what's fact and what's fiction when it comes to her relationship with her husband, Justin Bieber.

The model took to her Instagram Stories on March 5 and used a brief moment to clarify some of the recent gossip items about her and her husband, writing, "Just FYI, the stories and constant 'blind items' I see on TikTok are 100% of the time wrong. Made out of thin air… Come from the land of delusion…So I know it may be fun feeding into these stories but just know they’re always false xx sorry to spoil it."

Hailey's recent post comes amid rumors of her and Justin having relationship issues, with some people speculating that they are on track or planning to get divorced. Her father, Stephen Baldwin, added fuel to the fire after reposting an Instagram video to his feed by Victor Marx, founder of The All Things Possible Ministry, in which he asked his followers to pray for the couple.

"Christians, please when you think of Justin & Hailey, take a moment to offer a little prayer for them to have wisdom, protection and to draw close to the Lord," Marx shared in a Feb. 26 Instagram Reel. "There are special challenges that folks in high-visibility positions face." 

Hailey, however, debunked some of the speculation by the time Justin's 30th birthday rolled around this year. "Words could never truly describe the beauty of who you are," she wrote via Instagram on March 1. "Happy Birthday to you… love of my life, for life."
