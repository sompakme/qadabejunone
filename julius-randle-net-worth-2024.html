Early Life and Career

Julius Randle was born in Dallas, Texas, and developed a passion for basketball at a young age. He played college basketball for the Kentucky Wildcats before declaring for the NBA draft in 2014. The Los Angeles Lakers selected him with the seventh overall pick, and he played for the Lakers until 2018. He then signed with the New York Knicks in 2019, where he has been playing since.

Current Contract and Salary

In 2019, Randle signed a three-year contract with the New York Knicks worth $62.1 million. This contract significantly increased his annual salary, making him one of the highest-paid players on the Knicks roster.
