Tory Lanez Wiki, Biography, Net Worth, Age, Career, Relationship,Family

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Tory Lanez, also known as Tory Lanez in the professional world, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He was born on July 27, 1992, in Brampton, Canada, making him 30 years old as of 202Lanez gained his first notable recognition with the mixtape “Conflicts of My Soul: The 416 Story,” released in August 201Throughout his career, he has showcased his talents in the music industry, working his way up from mixtapes to studio albums.

Career Highlights:

  • Released his debut mixtape “T.L 2 T.O” in 2009.
  • Gained attention with music videos uploaded to his YouTube channel while residing in South Florida.
  • Caught the interest of Sean Kingston after a freestyle video, leading to performances and mixtape releases.
  • Signed a record deal with Kingston’s Time is Money Entertainment in 2011.
  • Released mixtapes like “Mr. Peterson,” “Chixtape,” and “Swavey.”
  • Transitioned to working as an independent musician and dropped mixtapes like “Sincerely Tory” and “Conflicts of My Soul: The 416 Story.”
  • Released “Chixtape II” in 2014 and collaborated on “Public Swave Announcement” episodes.

Studio Albums and Notable Achievements:

  • Released his first studio album, “I Told You,” in 2016, featuring hit singles “Say It” and “Luv.”
  • Followed by the release of his second and third studio albums, “Memories Don’t Die” and “Love Me Now?” in 2018.
  • Achieved Billboard Hot 100 success with singles like “Say It” and “Luv.”
  • His fourth studio album, “Chixtape 5,” peaked at number 2 on the US Billboard 200 in 2019.
  • Released his fifth studio album, “Daystar,” in 2020, featuring responses to the controversy of him shooting Megan Thee Stallion.

The Guilty Verdict and Legal Situation:

  • Tory Lanez faced legal troubles related to the shooting of fellow rapper Megan Thee Stallion in 2020.
  • On December 23, 2022, Lanez was found guilty on three counts, including carrying a loaded, unregistered firearm in a moving vehicle and grossly negligent discharge of a firearm.
  • The jury’s verdict could lead to a sentence of nearly 20 years behind bars for Lanez.
  • Megan Thee Stallion expressed her satisfaction with the verdict, stating that justice has been served.
