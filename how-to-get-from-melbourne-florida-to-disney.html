How To Get From Melbourne Florida To Disney

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 12, 2024
People are currently reading this guide.

Buckle Up, Buttercup: Your Epic Voyage from Melbourne to Disney World (Without Getting Lost in the Sunshine State Shuffleboard!)

Ah, Melbourne, Florida. Land of sunshine, surfboards...and surprisingly, not the home of Mickey Mouse. But fear not, fellow traveler! Disney World awaits, a mere 70-ish miles away. The question is, how do you get there without wrestling an alligator for a swamp buggy (don't worry, they're mostly chill)?

Here's your not-so-scientific guide to conquering the Melbourne-to-Disney divide:

Option 1: Chariot of Fire (Rental Car Edition)

  • Pros: Freedom! Blast your Disney tunes, sing along to show tunes at the top of your lungs (no judgement here), and make pit stops for roadside citrus stands (Florida oranges, anyone?).
  • Cons: Beware of the siren song of the GPS that might lead you down a random dirt road. Also, keep an eye out for those pesky tolls – they're like little gremlins waiting to snatch your quarters.
  • Bonus points: If you manage to navigate rush hour traffic without becoming a meme on I-95, you automatically qualify for a "Florida Driving Champion" badge (participation trophy not included).

Option 2: Knights of the Round Trip (Shuttle Service)

  • Pros: Sit back, relax, and let someone else worry about the driving. Catch up on your Disney movie marathon or plot your park strategy – the choice is yours!
  • Cons: Sharing is caring, and that means sharing the ride with fellow Disney-bound adventurers. Buckle up for singalongs from excited youngsters (it's all part of the charm, right?).
  • Bonus points: If you manage to snag the coveted shotgun seat, you get ultimate bragging rights (and maybe a slightly better view).

The Great Disney Debate: To Rent or Not to Rent?

Here's a lightning round to help you decide:

  • Planning on exploring beyond Disney? Rental car all the way!
  • Just here for the magic? Shuttles are your jam.
  • Feeling fancy? Hire a limo and pretend you're a celebrity (just don't forget the autograph book for Mickey!).

How to Get There From Here: Wrap Up

No matter your choice, the adventure awaits! So grab your Mickey ears, pack your sunscreen (Florida sun is no joke), and get ready for a sprinkle of pixie dust.

Bonus FAQ: How to Get to Disney World from Melbourne, Florida

  • How long does the drive take? The drive is about 1.5 hours, depending on traffic and your alligator wrangling skills (hopefully minimal).
  • Can I take a bus? There isn't a direct bus route, but you can take a shuttle service that combines a shuttle to the airport with a connecting bus to Disney.
  • Is Uber/Lyft an option? Yes, but for this distance, it might be pricier than other options.
  • What about a train? There's no train service between Melbourne and Disney World.
  • Should I bring snacks for the ride? Always! You never know when a case of the munchies might strike (or you need to bribe a sugar-crazed toddler with Mickey Mouse pretzels).
