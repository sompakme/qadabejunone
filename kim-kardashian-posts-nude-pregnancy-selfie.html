Kim Kardashian Posts Nude Pregnancy Selfie

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 5, 2024

Kim Kardashian West is pregnant, you guys. But not everyone seems to think that’s the case, so the reality star has taken to Instagram to prove her baby bump is the real deal.

Kanye West‘s other half posted a nude pregnancy selfie on Tuesday and captioned the shot, “First they say I’m too skinny so I have to be faking it…Now they say I’m too big so I have to be faking it…SMH! Some days I’m photographed before I eat & look smaller, some days I’ve just eaten & I look bigger. It’s all a part of the process.”

“I think you all know me well enough to know I would document the process if I got a surrogate,” she continued. “Everyone’s body is different, every pregnancy is very different! I’ve learned to love my body at every stage! I’m going to get even bigger & that’s beautiful too!”

And in case her explanation wasn’t enough, North West‘s mama made sure to add the hashtags “#NoFilter #NoPhotoShop #GoodLighting.”

While we’re already used to seeing the reality star stripped down, it’s her timing of the post that’s most interesting.

Following the news about a morning anchor storming off set after he refused to talk about Kylie Jenner‘s new bunny, Bruce, a #NoKardashiansforAWeek hashtag has circulated on Twitter. One of the biggest supporters of the online movement comes surprisingly from celebrity blogger Perez Hilton, who tweeted, “We will NOT be featuring any stories about the #Kardashians for the entire week of Aug 17-21!”

As for how Kardashian feels about all this, Hilton’s tweet below explains it all.

Kim just unfollowed me on Twitter. Oh well. I do love the Kardashians, but Kleanses are part of a healthy diet! #NoKardashiansForAWeek

— Perez Hilton (@PerezHilton) August 11, 2015
