Savagely Attack

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Give Savagely Attack a listen - it's a new offering from Czarface, featuring Ghostface Killah . It was dropped on Tuesday, February 19th, 2013. Czarface's musical situation is improving with every new release, and Savagely Attack will most definitely help the cause. It's a fine addition to the growing body of work Czarface has been developing since stepping foot in the game. We're looking forward to what Czarface has in store.

Have you heard Czarface's Let It Off (Prod. DJ Premier)? If your interest is piqued, hit up Czarface's profile for more info, news, songs, and mixtapes. Have you checked out Czarface's Twitter pages?

What do you think of the features on this track? How do you feel about the production here?
