List of Tour de France Winners

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 17, 2024

List of Tour de France Winners : In 2022, the 109th edition of the Cycling tour, Tour de France was held in Denmark on July 1. Tadej Pogačar again won the tour consecutively for the third time. The Tour was started in 1903, it’s been more than 100 years the tour is still organized. Throughout its timeline, the tour has seen many legendary winners, controversies, and whatnot. Let’s find out the list of winners and more to date.

Who has the most time in the Tour de France? 

France, Belgium, and Spain have the most wins with 36, 18 and 12 wins respectively. 

The facts about champions and championship 

The original winner of 1904 was Mauricio Garin, but he was caught by a train for a race that disqualified him. 

In 1996, the original winner Bjarne Riis was admitted to doping use, so he was exempted from the tournament. 

From 1999-2005, the tournament was held but the champion of these years, Lance Armstrong, was later caught doping. 

In 2010 and 2012, Albert Cantador was an initial winner but due to a drug investigation, he was stripped of the winning position. 

In 2006,  Floyd Landis, the initial winner, was stripped due to a failed drug test.
