Love is Blind star almost used another name for the show

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Kwame Appiah’s Journey from Soccer to Stardom

Kwame Appiah, the Love is Blind star from Season 4, has become a household name, but some fans still wonder what his full name is. Interestingly, he almost used another name for the show. Let’s delve into his background and the story behind his name.

From Soccer Field to Love Pod

At 33 years old, Kwame Appiah has had an interesting journey. He hails from Ghana and used to be a soccer player. He even played for teams like the Philadelphia Fury and the Delaware Stars before taking his talents to Sweden. After his soccer career, he transitioned into becoming a Sales Development Manager.

Connecting with Kwame on Instagram

If you want to keep up with Kwame’s life after his stint on Netflix, you can find him on Instagram @ayokwam.

Kwame’s Full Name Revelation

Now, let’s get to the juicy bit. What’s Kwame’s full name? Well, during Love is Blind Season 4, he dropped a bombshell. He revealed that his full name is Alex Kwame Owusu Ansah Appiah Junior. However, he almost went by the name ‘Alex’ instead of Kwame.

The Name Game Controversy

Kwame’s decision to almost use ‘Alex’ sparked some controversy among fans. He explained to Micah in the pods that he felt like ‘Kwame’ pigeonholed him into a specific category. This created a buzz on social media, and Kwame had to address it on his Instagram.

In his Instagram story, he clarified, “I was named after my now late uncle, ‘Alex Owusu Ansah,’ making me ‘Alex Kwame Owusu Ansah Appiah.’ Sometimes I use the name, Alex. A lot of people are wondering, so letting y’all know.”

 Love Still Blooms: Kwame and Chelsea’s Journey

Now, moving away from the name drama, what about Kwame’s relationship status? Well, at the Love is Blind live reunion, we found out that Kwame and Chelsea are still happily married after a year. Not only that, but they are currently residing together in Seattle.

A Year of Progress

Their relationship has evolved significantly since the show. During the reunion, they revealed that Chelsea had met Kwame’s mom, a milestone that didn’t happen on the show. Despite some fans commenting on their lack of chemistry, Kwame set the record straight on his Instagram.

“Based on what you saw, I don’t blame you for thinking that. But actually, we had the MOST fun together falling in love!” he proclaimed.

In summary, Kwame Appiah, the Love is Blind star, has a full name that almost wasn’t used on the show. He’s a former soccer player with an exciting career path, now working as a Sales Development Manager. You can keep up with his life on Instagram. And most importantly, his love story with Chelsea is still going strong, with their relationship reaching new milestones after the show’s conclusion. Love truly knows no bounds for Kwame and Chelsea!
