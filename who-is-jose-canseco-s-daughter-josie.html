Who is Jose Canseco's daughter, Josie?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 10, 2024

JOSE Canseco rose to fame in the 1990s thanks to his career with the Oakland Athletics.

During that time, the former MLB player welcomed one daughter, Josie, and now fans want to know more about her.

Who is Jose Canseco's daughter Josie?

Josie Canseco was born in 1996 to Jose and former model Jessica Sekely.

Outside of being known as Jose's daughter, she is known for her career as a model and social media influencer.

Over the years, Josie has also dabbled in the television industry and was previously seen on VH1's Hollywood Exes and in the internet web series SummerBreak 2.

When she's not working or walking in the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show, she can be found posting about her life on Instagram to over 1.2million followers.

What is Josie Canseco's net worth?

Due to Josie's successful career in the entertainment industry, she has been able to amass a fortune.

As of March 2023, her net worth is an estimated $2million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This estimate comes from her modeling earnings as well as other business ventures.

While some fans might think that her worth stems from her father's MLB career, she has since taken to social media to set the record straight.

In March 2023, she posted a series of Instagram pics claiming that her career is self-made and that her "family is broke."

"Everyone claiming I came from all this rich money because I'm a 'canseco' do ur research," she wrote, via TMZ.

"My 'family money' was blown by the time I was 6/7 w bankruptcy n I had to work my ass off to not only sustain my 'family name' but also pay my bills cause no one paid that s*** except me."

"My family is broke," she continued, "and I dont get the advance of a family that was rich."

Josie went on to add that while she has "been graced with a privilege of being born within a family that earned what they earned," she "really went through that s*** n busted my a** the traditional way."

TMZ notes that Jose made over $40million in his MLB career but previously filed for bankruptcy in 2012.

At this time, it is unclear how Jose lost all of his baseball earnings.

Is Josie Canseco single?

Since stepping into the spotlight, Josie has been linked to several high-profile guys.

Her first public relationship was with hip-hop artist Mike Stud, which lasted from 2015 to 2019.

She then dated Brody Jenner in 2019 and Logan Paul in 2020.
