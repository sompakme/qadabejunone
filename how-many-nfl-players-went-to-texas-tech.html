How Many Nfl Players Went To Texas Tech

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 30, 2024
People are currently reading this guide.

Texas Tech: Powerhouse of Pigskin… Alumni-wise at Least!

So, you're curious about how many NFL players hail from the land of tortillas, tumbleweeds, and the Texas Tech Red Raiders? Well, buckle up, buttercup, because we're about to dive into the fascinating world of Red Raider gridiron alumni (and maybe uncover a few hilarious facts along the way).

Drafted vs. Played: A Numbers Game for the Statistically Obsessed

Now, statistics can get a tad dry, but here's the gist: according to the folks at Wikipedia (those glorious guardians of random knowledge), 155 Texas Tech players have been drafted into the NFL since the league's drafting days began in 1936. That's a respectable number, folks.

But hold on to your ten-gallon hats! That number only reflects drafted players. There are plenty of Red Raiders who suited up for the NFL without getting the official draft nod. Tracking down those numbers is like wrangling a greased pig – nearly impossible! So, let's just say the true number of Texas Tech NFL alumni is likely higher than a cheerleader's hair after a touchdown.

From Number One Picks to Undrafted Gems: A Celebration of Red Raider Resilience

Texas Tech boasts a legendary number one draft pick: Dave Parks, snagged by the Green Bay Packers in 1964. This guy paved the way for future Red Raider hopefuls. But let's not forget the undrafted heroes who clawed their way onto NFL rosters with pure grit and talent. These guys are the real Rocky Balboas of the football world, proving that sometimes, heart trumps draft position.

Texas Tech in the NFL: More Than Just Flash in the Pan

Sure, Texas Tech might not be the first school that comes to mind when you think NFL powerhouses. But these Red Raiders have carved their niche in the league. We've got Super Bowl champions, Pro Bowlers, and enough highlight-reel catches to make your jaw drop. Michael Crabtree, anyone? Dude was a touchdown machine!

The Wrap-Up: Texas Tech – Breeding Ground for NFL Talent (and Maybe a Few Laughs)

So, there you have it. Texas Tech might not be churning out NFL stars like a bakery cranks out donuts, but they've definitely got a respectable showing. Who knows, maybe someday we'll see a whole stampede of Red Raiders leading their teams to victory. One thing's for sure, though: Texas Tech football is a guaranteed source of entertainment, both on and off the field. Heck, just watching those bright red uniforms zipping around the field is enough to put a smile on your face (or maybe that's just the Texas heat getting to you).
