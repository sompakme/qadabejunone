Who is Devin Booker Dating? A Look at His Love Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Devin Booker stands out as one of the most accomplished players in the NBA, showcasing his impressive skills as the Phoenix Suns’ shooting guard. Recognized for his scoring prowess, clutch performances, and leadership, Booker has earned two All-Star appearances and notably led the Suns to their first NBA Finals in 28 years in 2021.

In addition to his basketball success, Booker has gained attention for his personal life. Renowned for his charm and good looks, he has been romantically linked to several high-profile women. Currently, he is in a relationship with Kendall Jenner, a supermodel and reality TV star from the Kardashian-Jenner family. Their relationship began in April 2020, and they officially confirmed their romance on Valentine’s Day in 2021.

Exploring Devin Booker’s Romantic Journey

1. Relationship with Jordyn Woods:

Devin Booker’s initial publicized relationship was with Jordyn Woods, a model and social media influencer known for her connection to Kylie Jenner. The couple, who started dating in 2018, maintained a casual and low-key relationship. Despite being spotted together at various events, they opted not to make their relationship official or exclusive.

Facing rumors and drama surrounding cheating allegations, a Kylie Jenner feud, and a Tristan Thompson scandal, Booker and Woods eventually parted ways in 2019. The breakup was not officially announced, but both unfollowed each other on Instagram, signaling the end of their relationship. Following the split, Devin Booker began dating Kendall Jenner, while Jordyn Woods found companionship with NBA player Karl-Anthony Towns.

2. Meeting Kendall Jenner:

Devin Booker and Kendall Jenner’s paths crossed in early 2020 through mutual friends. Sharing the same social circle, they grew closer during the COVID-19 pandemic, culminating in a road trip to Arizona in April 2020. The couple attended a Memorial Day party together, sparking rumors of a budding romance.

Keeping their relationship private initially, Booker and Jenner officially confirmed their status on Valentine’s Day 2021 through Instagram posts capturing their affection. They made their red carpet debut at the Met Gala in September 2021, marking a public celebration of their relationship.

3. Relationship Highlights:

Since confirming their romance, Devin Booker and Kendall Jenner have openly shared moments of their relationship on social media. From traveling and celebrating to supporting each other’s careers, the couple has become a beloved pairing. Devin Booker has been present at Kendall Jenner’s New York Fashion Week runway appearances, while she, in turn, has cheered him on during his NBA Finals performances.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, Devin Booker and Kendall Jenner’s relationship has flourished since its inception in early 2020. The couple, now a powerful and affectionate duo, officially acknowledged their love in 2021 and continue to captivate fans with glimpses into their journey together.
