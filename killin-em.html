Killin' Em

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 1, 2024

Stream Killin' Em, the newest drop from Chris Webby. The cut was released on Wednesday, June 1st, 2011. Things are looking better for Chris Webby with each new release, which Killin' Em has proven yet again. It's worthy addition to the impressive body of work Chris Webby has been developing over the years. We're looking forward to hearing what's next.

Hear more of Chris Webby on What I Do. If you're down, make sure to visit Chris Webby's profile page for the newest info, news, songs, and mixtapes. Make sure to hit Chris Webby with a follow and/or like on Facebook and Twitter.
