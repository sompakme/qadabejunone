Drake's baby's mom Sophie Brussaux sweetly admired on his Instagram

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Drake recently shared a family snap via Instagram which included his baby’s mom Sophie Brussaux as the co-parents celebrated their child’s birthday.

Sophie Brussaux gave birth to Adonis Graham, the son of Canadian rapper and singer Drake, on 2017. Having recently celebrated his sixth birthday, the celebrity parents have been celebrating him on social media.

Drake features baby’s mom in Instagram snap

Born on 24 October 1986 in Toronto, Canada, Aubrey Drake Graham is now age 36 years and is the father to his son Adonis Graham.

On Wednesday 11 October, Drake shared an Instagram story snap of him and some family members celebrating Adonis’ sixth birthday.

Also posing in the photograph is mother and French artist Sophie Brussaux, who is stood next to Drake with their son in front of them.

A Twitter fan page dedicated to posting Drake content re-shared the rapper’s Instagram story in a tweet:

Drake shocked some fans when his baby’s mom made an appearance alongside their kid as the three of them aren’t photographed together all that often.

All about artist Sophie Brussaux

Sophie Brussaux gave birth to their child on October 11 2017, and it is reported that the pair met in early 2017, as per Capital Xtra.

Brussaux was first linked with the rapper in January 2017 after the couple were photographed on a dinner date in Amsterdam while Drake was stopping over on his Boy Meets World Tour.

Currently 34 years old, the artist was born on August 1, 1989.

Previously working under the stage name ‘Rosee Divine’, Sophie Brussaux now works as an artist and painter.

Brussaux can be found on Instagram as @sophieknowsbetter, where she shares photos of Adonis as well as images of her art and luxury lifestyle.

The creator’s bio is currently dedicated to her son, reading: “Mom to the greatest little human in the known universe and beyond.”

On the day of Adonis’ birthday, Sophie Brussaux, shared an adorable Instagram carousel post to celebrate her and Drake’s son turning age six.

Another Instagram account with the name @sophieknowsart is used for her artwork.

The proud mother has also featured her son on other social media platforms, including a popular video on her TikTok page (@sophieknowsbetter) filled with memories captured from the family’s European summer holidays.

Over on Twitter, the creator be found as @sophiebrussaux. Her bio currently describes her as an ‘Artiste peintre and an ‘activist’ while her location is set to Toronto, Ontario, the most populous city in Canada.
