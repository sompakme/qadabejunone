McMahon wasn't without controversy. The 1990s saw a shift to edgier content, which led to clashes with conservative groups and regulatory bodies. The "Attitude Era" pushed boundaries, garnering high ratings but also sparking debates about appropriateness in mainstream media.

His controversies extend beyond the "Attitude Era." In June 2022, he stepped down from his position as CEO and chairman of WWE after accusations emerged of hush-money agreements with women that he allegedly had affairs with. He has also faced a number of allegations surrounding sexual misconduct, going as far back as 1992.
