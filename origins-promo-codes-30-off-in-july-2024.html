FAQs

Does Origins offer free shipping?

Yes, Origins offer free shipping for orders over $50. Alternatively, Standard shipping for orders under this amount costs $7, whilst 2-Day shipping costs $15 and Overnight Shipping costs $25. 

Can I earn Origins rewards?

Yes, customers who sign up for My Origins Rewards will receive 1 point for every $1 spent. You can then enjoy perks such as a welcome offer, loyalty cash, free shipping on every order, free birthday gifts, free samples, and more. 

Is there an Origins email newsletter?

Yes, customers who sign up for the Origins email newsletter will receive a 15% off Origins promo code for their next order. Not only will you make some savings, but you'll also be the first to know about the latest products, sales, offers, new, and more.

Can I get an Origins student discount?

Absolutely. Both students and teachers can get a 15% Origins coupon code. To access this saving you will need to verify your academic status with SheerID. Once approved you can shop to your heart's content and enjoy your discount.

Does Origins offer a free gift?

Yes, Origins often offers a free gift to customers who spend over a certain amount. For example, they are currently offering a Double Cleanse Kit (consisting of a Cheek & Balances Milky Oil Cleanser, a face wash by the same brand, and a headband) when you spend over $80. These offers change regularly, so grab them while you can. 

What is the Origins returns policy?

If you would like to return your order, you have up to 60 days to send it back in a new or slightly used condition to receive a refund. If your item came with a free gift, they must be returned together to be eligible for a refund or exchange. Please note that final sale items are unable to be returned.
