Joan Rivers’ Career Highlights

Joan Rivers’ career was marked by numerous highlights that not only built her reputation as a comedian but also contributed significantly to her net worth.

  • Television Appearances: Rivers was a regular guest on “The Tonight Show” and eventually became the first woman to host a late-night network television talk show with “The Late Show Starring Joan Rivers.”
  • Comedy Albums and Specials: Her comedy albums and specials were critically acclaimed and commercially successful.
  • Books: Rivers was a bestselling author, with several books to her name, including memoirs and humor books.
  • Fashion Line: She launched a successful fashion jewelry line on QVC, which became a significant source of income.
  • Reality TV: Rivers appeared in and won “Celebrity Apprentice” and starred in the reality TV series “Joan & Melissa: Joan Knows Best?” with her daughter, Melissa Rivers.
