Mo Cheddah and Husband Welcome First Child After Years of Marriage, Singer Shares Stunning Baby Bump

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 15, 2024
  • Popular Nigerian singer and lifestyle blogger, Mo Cheddah, and her husband are now first-time parents
  • The music star and her politician husband, Prince Bukunyi Olateru-Olagbegi, recently welcomed a baby and shared the news online
  • Mo Cheddah shared stunning baby bump photos as fans gushed over the latest addition to her family

Talented Nigerian musician, Mo Cheddah, has shared the great news of her newfound status as a mother on social media.

The songstress and lifestyle blogger who kept a low profile on social media for a while finally broke the news of her motherhood to her numerous fans.

Taking to her verified Instagram page, Mo Cheddah shared a series of stunning photos and videos of herself rocking her baby bump.

In one of the posts, Mo posted a photo of herself with her politician husband, Prince Bukunyi Olateru-Olagbegi, as well as an adorable video before she noted that they were now parents with a simple caption.

Actress Bimbo Success welcomes twins after years of marriage

It was good news for the Yoruba Nollywood industry as actress Bimbo Success welcomed her first set of twins.

Bimbo married her partner, Oscar, a renowned Yoruba movie director in 2016 in an elaborate wedding ceremony and they have now welcomed their first children together.

Bimbo's colleague, Iyabo Ojo made the announcement of the babies' arrival on her verified Instagram page.

Iyabo shared a photo collage of the new mother and her twins with Adeyinka Alaseyori's song playing in the background.
