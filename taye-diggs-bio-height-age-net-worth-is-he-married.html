Other popular remarkable performances of Taye Diggs are Seen in The Wood, Days of Wrath, Cake and Chicago. He, at one time, was a dancer in Tokyo Disneyland.

He made his television debut in a 1996 episode of New York Undercover as Stephon. He later featured in Private Practice where he acted the role of Dr. Sam Bennett. From 2014 through 2016, the actor played an inspector role in the show Murder in the First. Other movies that he was featured include The Best Man, and The Best Man Holiday. Taye has taken singing jobs for different Broadway shows.

Taye Diggs age

Taye Diggs was born and raised in Newark, New Jersey, on January 2nd, 1971. He is currently 48 years old.

Where is Taye Diggs from?

Taye Diggs was named Scott Leo at birth by his parents Andre Young and Marcia Berry. His parents had five children, Taye being the firstborn. Later, his mother was married to his stepfather Jeffries Diggs who gave Taye his surname. He went to school at the Allendale Columbia School and later on joined art school both located in Rochester, New York. He went to the Syracuse University where he graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA).

Taye Diggs movies

Taye Diggs started his acting carrier in the year 1996; he appeared in Rent where he played a mean and expedient landlord. His now ex-wife Idina Menzel was also featured in the same movie. After successfully being featured in different roles on stage, he switched to TV in 1998. He began to act in a soap opera known as The Guiding Light, which was awarded a Guinness World Record for running the longest on TV in the American television history. Also, in 1998, he featured in a comedy movie based on a best-selling book by Terry McMillan. The character he played was nominated for the Best Actor in the Acapulco Black Film Festival Award.
