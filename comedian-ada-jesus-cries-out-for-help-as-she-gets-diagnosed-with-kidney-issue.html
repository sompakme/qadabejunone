Comedian Ada Jesus cries out for help as she gets diagnosed with kidney issue

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

- A young woman Ella Ada recently shared a piece of news about her friend Ada Jesus

- Ella reported that the comedian is down with a kidney problem and needs money

- Ada's friend also shared photos of the comedian laying on a hospital bed

A comedian Mmesoma Mercy Obi popularly called Ada Jesus is critically ill as her friends and family have taken to social media to call for help.

According to a post by one lady called Ella Ada on Facebook, Ada Jesus has a kidney problem and has been admitted to the hospital.

Ella called on her fans and followers to pray that nothing negative happens to the comedian.
