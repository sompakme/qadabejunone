One Piece Chapter 1098: One Piece is one of the most popular and longest-running manga series in the world, created by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew of pirates, who are searching for the legendary treasure One Piece and pursuing their dreams. The manga has been serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump magazine since 1997 and has over 490 million copies in circulation worldwide. The anime adaptation, produced by Toei Animation, has been airing since 1999 and has over 1000 episodes.

Fans are eagerly waiting for the next chapter of One Piece, Chapter 1098, to find out what will happen next in this flashback arc. Will Kuma be able to save Lindbergh and return to Ginny? Recap

The following is a brief summary of the events that took place in One Piece Chapter 1097. For a more detailed recap, fans can read the full chapter on the official platforms mentioned above.

The chapter begins with a flashback to 8 years after the God Valley Incident, where Kuma and Ginny are living in the Sorbet Kingdom in South Blue. Kuma is a pastor who uses his Paw-Paw fruit powers to heal the people’s pain and suffering, while Ginny is a leader of the resistance against the tyrannical King Bekori, who oppresses the citizens and demands tribute from them. Kuma and Ginny have a close relationship, but Kuma refuses Ginny’s marriage proposal, saying that he does not want to burden her with his past as a slave of the Celestial Dragons.

Kuma reveals his dream of becoming a hero at sea, like his idol Monkey D. Dragon, the leader of the Revolutionary Army. He says that he wants to help people who are oppressed and discriminated by the World Government like he and Ginny were. Ginny supports his dream but asks him to stay with her in the Sorbet Kingdom.

The chapter then jumps to 5 years later, where Kuma and Ginny are part of the Revolutionary Army, along with other comrades such as Sabo, Koala, Hack, Morley, Belo Betty, Karasu, and Lindbergh. They are on a mission to infiltrate a World Government facility and rescue some prisoners. However, they are ambushed by the Cipher Pol agents, who capture Lindbergh and escape. Kuma decides to go after them, despite Ginny’s protests and leaves the Sorbet Kingdom with a group of revolutionaries.

The chapter ends with a cliffhanger, as Kuma and his allies encounter a mysterious and powerful enemy, who claims to be the “true king” of the Sorbet Kingdom. The enemy has a scarred face and a crown, and says that he has been waiting for Kuma to return. He also says that he knows Kuma’s true identity and his connection to the Celestial Dragons. He then attacks Kuma with a blast of fire, leaving his fate unknown.
