Olivia Muenter - InStyle

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 21, 2024

Title: Contributing Writer

Education: University of Florida

Location: New York, New York

Expertise: Beauty, Fashion

  • Olivia Muenter is a writer and editor based in New York who covers all things fashion and beauty.
  • She's been published in Glamour, InStyle, Apartment Therapy, Woman's Day, Elite Daily, and other publications.
  • Previously she was a full-time senior fashion & beauty editor for Bustle for four years.

Experience

Olivia started her editorial career as an intern at College Fashionista, where she worked her way up to Head Style Guru. She worked at Bustle for five years, first as a contributing writer and then as the full-time Senior Fashion & Beauty Editor. She is currently a full-time freelance beauty and fashion writer. Her work has been published in Glamour, InStyle, Apartment Therapy, Woman's Day, Elite Daily, and other publications.

Education

Olivia graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Journalism from the University of Florida.

About InStyle

InStyle is an award-winning destination for fashion, beauty, and pop-culture, with an all-are-welcome ethos that swings open the proverbial velvet ropes. Whether you’re looking for a wardrobe refresh, haircut inspiration, or a story that’ll make sparkling cocktail-party conversation, our expert-connected coverage is where to start.
