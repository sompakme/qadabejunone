What is the appearance of annual bluegrass?

Known by the scientific name annual bluegrass (Poa annua), this cool-season annual grass grows by seed. Annual bluegrass has a tufted growth style with brilliant green leaves that have a fine texture and a bright green tint. The leaf blades of annual bluegrass are smooth and have a boat-shaped tip.

What is the best way to get rid of annual meadow grass?

If Annual Meadow Grass is present in tiny numbers, physical removal with a sharp knife is the most effective method of removing it from the environment. Cut into the roots with a forceful and diagonal motion. Because Annual Meadow Grass does not have subterranean stems, it will die if its roots are cut or otherwise removed from the ground (provided all roots have been removed).
